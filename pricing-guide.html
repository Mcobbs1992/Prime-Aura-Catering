Pricing Guideline Booklet

A transparent overview of our packages, what’s included, and how pricing works.

1. Our Approach to Pricing

Prime Aura Catering is designed to feel simple, transparent, and high-end. We build packages that cover the core needs of most events while allowing room for customization and upgrades.

  • Clear base pricing for 125 guests
  • All-in buffet setup with disposable wares
  • Signature dishes included in every package

2. Base Package Overview (125 Guests)

Package Includes Starting Price
Southern Soul Buffet Egg rolls, 1 entrée, 2 sides, rolls, disposables, setup. $1,400
Signature Dinner Buffet Egg rolls, 2 entrées, 2 sides, disposables, setup. $1,500
Premium Upscale Buffet Egg rolls, appetizers, 2 entrées, 3 sides, dessert, disposables, setup. $1,650

Pricing may adjust slightly based on menu customization, ingredient costs, and event logistics.

3. What’s Included in Every Package

  • Menu planning & preparation
  • Buffet-style food presentation
  • Delivery to event location (within agreed radius)
  • Disposable plates, cutlery, napkins
  • Chafing dishes & fuel for hot items

4. Optional Add-Ons

These are sample ranges you can adjust as needed:

  • Additional appetizer selection: $150–$300+
  • Additional dessert selection: $125–$250+
  • Staffed buffet service (servers): $25–$40 per hour per staff member
  • Custom menu design or tasting: Quoted per event

5. Payment, Deposits & Policies

  • Deposit: 50% due at booking to secure date.
  • Final Payment: Due 5 days prior to event.
  • Accepted Methods: Cash, card, Zelle, Cash App.
  • Cancellations: 7+ days’ notice for partial refund of deposit.

6. Custom Events & Larger Guest Counts

For events with a different guest count, plated service, or unique menu requests, we’ll provide a custom quote. The best way to begin is to share:

  • Expected guest count
  • Event date & venue
  • Preferred style (buffet, plated, family-style)
  • Any must-have dishes or themes
